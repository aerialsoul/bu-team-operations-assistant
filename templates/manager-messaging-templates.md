## HOW THIS FILE WORKS

BU provides official templates for common situations.
Many managers customize these templates over time based
on their own experience and communication style.

This file is the place to store those customizations so
the assistant can use them consistently.

### Guidelines for Customizing Templates:
- Maintain professional tone
- Do not remove important policy language
- Do not commit to anything outside BU policy
- Keep the core message and intent of the original
- Personalization is encouraged - these are your words

### When to Add a Template Here:
- You've customized an official BU template
- You've created a message that worked well and 
  want to reuse it
- The assistant drafts something you like - 
  ask it to save it here for future use

## TEMPLATE 3: RESPONSE TO OPPONENT RESCHEDULE REQUEST

**Based on:** Official BU Response Template
**Last Used:** [TBD]
**Customized:** [Yes | No]

**When to use:**
Opponent has requested to reschedule a game (usually 
an away game for BU). Responding to their request.

### 3a - Accepting the Request:

**Template Text:**

    Subject: Re: [Match #XXXXX] Reschedule Request - 
    [BU Team] vs [Opponent Team]

    Hi [Opponent Manager Name],

    Thanks for reaching out! [Proposed date] works for 
    our team. We're happy to move the game to that date.

    Please go ahead and submit the reschedule request 
    with your club and let us know once it's updated 
    in GotSport.

    Looking forward to the game!

    [Your Name]
    [BU Team] Manager

### 3b - Declining / Counter-Proposing:

**Template Text:**

    Subject: Re: [Match #XXXXX] Reschedule Request - 
    [BU Team] vs [Opponent Team]

    doesn't work for our team due to [brief reason].

    Would either of these dates work for you instead?
    - Option 1: [Day, Date]
    - Option 2: [Day, Date]

    Happy to find a date that works for both teams.
