## SAT / ACT TEST DAY CONSTRAINTS

### Which Teams Are Affected:
High school aged teams ONLY: **U15 and older (born before 08/01/2012)**
- These teams cannot start games before 3:00 PM on SAT/ACT test days
- Younger teams (U14 and below) are NOT affected by this constraint
- When in doubt about a team's age group, check bu-game-durations.md

### Why This Matters:
Many players on high school aged teams will be taking the SAT or ACT 
on these dates. Starting games before 3:00 PM risks players missing 
their test or missing the game. The 3:00 PM start rule gives students 
time to complete their test and travel to the game.

## FIELD ACCESS CONSTRAINTS

### Overview:
BU shares several fields with other sports and other clubs.
Having an unscheduled slot does not mean BU has access to that field.
Always verify field availability against bu-field-access.md before 
proposing home game times.

### Key Principle:
**Never suggest a home game time without confirming BU has 
field access during that window in bu-field-access.md.**

### Common Conflict Types:
- Other sports using the field (football season overlaps Fall soccer)
- Other clubs with shared field agreements
- School events taking priority over BU field access
- Maintenance or facility blackout dates

Full field access details are in bu-field-access.md.

## TOURNAMENT WEEKENDS

### Overview:
BU teams participate in tournaments throughout the season.
BU does not host tournaments, but coaches frequently take one 
or more of their teams to tournaments on weekend dates.

### Impact on Scheduling:
Tournament weekends create two specific challenges:

1. **Coach Availability:**
   If a coach has one of their other teams in a tournament, 
   they may be unavailable for league games that weekend.
   Check coach-NAME-profile.md and coach-NAME-schedule.md 
   for known tournament commitments.

2. **Guest Coach Availability:**
   On tournament weekends, other BU coaches are often busy 
   with their own tournament teams. This makes it harder to 
   find guest coaches if needed.
   ⚠️ When a tournament weekend is identified, flag early 
   that guest coach options may be limited.

### How to Handle Tournament Weekends:
- When a tournament is added to a coach's schedule, update 
  coach-NAME-profile.md with the dates and location
- Flag any league games that weekend as potentially needing 
  guest coach coverage
- Remind manager to arrange guest coach earlier than usual 
  (not just 1 week out) since availability will be tighter
- Check if any guest player source teams are also in the 
  tournament - they may be unavailable as guests
