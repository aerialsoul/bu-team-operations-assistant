## GOTSPORT EXPORT FILE TYPES
There are three schedule export files used by this assistant.
All three should be downloaded from the GotSport event pages 
and uploaded to update the club schedule.

1. **Main League Export** - Covers State Premier, Gold, Silver, 
   Bronze, and Copper division games. Largest file.
2. **NPL Export** - Covers NPL (National Premier League) division games.
3. **State Cup Export** - Covers State Cup tournament games.

### Who Can Export:
- The BU Director of Operations has single-click access to 
  full club exports for all three file types.
- Individual managers can export their own team's schedule 
  from their team's GotSport page.
- Full club exports (all teams) are preferred for field 
  coordination analysis.

## KNOWN CHANGES FOR FALL 2026

- **New age group matrix.** NorCal published a 2026/27 age group
  matrix. There is no U18; U19 is a two-year band. Age groups are
  set by birth-date cutoff (August 1), not by birth year subtraction.
  See bu-game-durations.md.
- **N1L replaces NPL for all BU teams** this season. There is no NPL
  for Fall 2026. Use event ID 54043 for N1L schedule exports.
- **Team naming may change.** NorCal has asked clubs to standardize
  GotSport team naming. BU may move from birth-year names

  confirmed as of July 8, 2026.
