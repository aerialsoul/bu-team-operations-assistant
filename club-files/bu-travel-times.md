## IMPORTANT NOTES ON TRAVEL TIMES

- All times assume normal weekend traffic conditions
- Weekend morning traffic is generally lighter than weekday
- Late afternoon games (4pm+) may encounter heavier traffic
- Always add buffer time for parking and walking to field
- When in doubt, add 10 min to estimates
- Travel times are approximate - recommend verifying 
  for critical same-day transitions

## TRAVEL FEASIBILITY RATINGS

When assessing if a coach can make it between games, factor in:
1. Game end time at current location
2. Travel time to next location
3. Coach's warmup time preference (default 45 min)
4. Buffer for parking and walking to field (~5-10 min)

### Rating Guide:
✅ COMFORTABLE: Arrives 15+ min before warmup needs to start
⚠️ TIGHT: Arrives within warmup window but cutting it close
🔴 IMPOSSIBLE: Cannot arrive before game starts
